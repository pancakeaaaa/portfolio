The Publishing Gateway: FANFICS TO BOOKS

My first ever publishing projects: From the trenches of Fan Fiction Websites to a literal book. Like many others in my generation, the beginning of my creative writing journey began with writing fan fiction on Archive of Our Own (Hereby, abbreviated to Ao3). Most fan fiction is published on websites such as Ao3 with just straight text in mind. CONSENT IS KEY!!!! Following similar parameters of the Zine Code of Ethics (CHICAGO CITATION), I made sure to contact each owner through social media to obtain consent (these are strictly personal projects AKA non-distributive).

Since it is a fan fiction, void of formalities of having a blurb, I combined the song I most associated with it, First Love Late Spring by Mitski (angsty, I know. I was a teenager haha).
